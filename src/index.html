Climate Change

“Global warming is the long-term heating of Earth’s climate system observed since the pre-industrial period (between 1850 and 1900) due to human activities, primarily fossil fuel burning, which increases heat-trapping greenhouse gas levels in Earth’s atmosphere.” -NASA

Climate change is caused by global warming. Climate data indicates that climate change has resulted in the increase of global land and ocean temperature; rising sea levels; ice loss at Earth’s poles and in mountain glaciers; and frequency and severity changes in extreme weather (i.e., natural disasters). -NASA
